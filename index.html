Accra, Ghana

Ghana is located in West Africa, is known for its rich history and vibrant culture.
It was the first African nation to gain independence from colonial rule in 1957, led by Kwame Nkrumah.
The country is famous for its diverse landscapes, ranging from coastal beaches to lush rainforests and savannahs.
Ghana is a hub for cocoa production, being one of the world’s leading exporters.
The capital, Accra, is a bustling city that reflects the country’s blend of tradition and modernity. Known for its warm hospitality.
